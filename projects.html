How2INFORM

View the website: H2i.info

How2INFORM focuses on providing media literacy tools and promoting critical thinking to help stop the spread of harmful content online. I took on this project in 2023, and the contract will run until October 2025. Originally created by a group of students in 2021, I have since been updating and redesigning the website to meet the client’s evolving needs. This project holds great significance for me, as I’m passionate about its mission and it has allowed me to further develop my skills independently. I truly enjoy the process of researching and leading a project on my own.
